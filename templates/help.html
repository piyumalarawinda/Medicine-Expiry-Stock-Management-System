Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medicine batch? expand_more

Navigate to the Inventory tab, click "Add New Stock", and scan the barcode or manually enter the batch details. Ensure you verify the expiry date before saving.

What do I do if stock counts don't match? expand_more

Perform a manual stocktake for the specific item. Use the "Adjust Stock" feature in the item details page to correct the count, adding a note for the discrepancy reason.

Can I undo a sale transaction? expand_more

Yes, within 24 hours. Go to "Transaction History", find the sale ID, and click "Void". For older transactions, you will need manager approval to process a return.
